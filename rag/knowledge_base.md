# AutoStream Knowledge Base

## About AutoStream
AutoStream is a SaaS platform that provides AI-powered automated video editing tools 
for content creators, marketers, and businesses. Our AI handles cutting, captioning, 
color correction, and publishing workflows automatically.

## Pricing Plans

### Basic Plan
- **Price**: $29/month
- **Videos**: Up to 10 videos per month
- **Resolution**: Up to 720p
- **AI Captions**: Not included
- **Support**: Standard email support (business hours only)
- **Best for**: Individual creators getting started with automated video editing

### Pro Plan
- **Price**: $79/month
- **Videos**: Unlimited videos per month
- **Resolution**: Up to 4K Ultra HD
- **AI Captions**: Included
- **Support**: 24/7 priority support
- **Best for**: Professional creators, marketing teams, and high-volume businesses

## Refund Policy
- No refunds are issued after 7 days of purchase
- Refund requests must be raised within the first 7 days
- Contact support@autostream.io for refund requests within the eligible window

## Support Policy
- 24/7 priority support is available exclusively on the Pro plan
- Basic plan users receive standard support during business hours only
- Support contact: support@autostream.io

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I upgrade from Basic to Pro?
Yes, you can upgrade anytime from your account dashboard.

### Can I cancel anytime?
Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. Access continues until 
the end of your current billing period.

### What platforms does AutoStream support?
AutoStream works for creators on YouTube, Instagram, TikTok, and LinkedIn.
